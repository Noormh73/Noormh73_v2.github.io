Located between Kyushu and Taiwan, Okinawa is made up of 160 Ryukyu Islands of various size between the East China Sea and The Philippine Sea. The islands are located in the subtropical zone with mild winters, and long, moist and rainy summers. With such conducive climate, it is obvious that Okinawa is a riot of colours as tropical flowers bloom all year round. One can also find rare flora and fauna around the islands.
The islands are also surrounded by crystal clear water and the shores are filled with endless stretches of soft white sand.
