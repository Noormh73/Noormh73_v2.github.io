I am But a Nomad in Search of Knowledge


Mohammad Noor Mohamed Hussein


“The education of a man is never completed until he dies.” ― Robert E. Lee


The constant search for knowledge is something I truly believe in. Thus, I believe that I should nurture the young minds and allow them to realise that knowledge serves as a beacon that could guide them to life's lessons.

I am an educator with Applied Linguistics background who has been teaching for the past 18 years. Throughout my journey in pedagogical practice, I have witnessed the rapid development of curriculum practices through information and communications technology. This development is one of the reasons which motivates me to pursue deeper understanding of information and communications technology.

Thus, with some understanding of coding, it will help me provide my students an opportunity to explore and understand the demands of the future economy.
